# Side Effects of Sugar Consumption
## Short-Term Effects
### Acute Intoxication
#### Symptoms
##### Headaches
##### Dizziness
##### Nausea and vomiting
##### Rapid heartbeat
##### Increased blood pressure
#### Causes
##### Consuming high amounts of sugar in a short period
##### Lack of insulin regulation
### Other Short-Term Effects
#### Hyperglycemia
##### High blood sugar levels
##### Dehydration
##### Fatigue
##### Blurred vision
#### Hypoglycemia
##### Low blood sugar levels
##### Shakiness and tremors
##### Sweating
##### Hunger
## Long-Term Effects
### Metabolic Disorders
#### Insulin Resistance
##### Difficulty regulating blood sugar levels
##### Increased risk of developing type 2 diabetes
#### Type 2 Diabetes
##### High blood sugar levels over time
##### Increased risk of cardiovascular disease, kidney damage, and nerve damage
### Cardiovascular Disease
#### Increased Risk
##### High blood pressure
##### High cholesterol levels
##### Atherosclerosis (hardening of the arteries)
### Other Long-Term Effects
#### Weight Gain and Obesity
##### Consuming more calories than needed
##### Increased risk of various health problems
#### Dental Caries and Gum Disease
##### Bacteria in the mouth feed on sugar
##### Increased risk of tooth decay and gum disease

##### Eye problems

##### Anemia
